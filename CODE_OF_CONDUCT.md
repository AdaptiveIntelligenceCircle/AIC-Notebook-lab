AIC-Lab is an open research project, aiming to build a transparent, safe and responsible Adaptive Intelligence platform. All members of the community are expected to adhere to the following principles.

## 1. Respect & Civility
- Always communicate honestly and professionally.

- Avoid all forms of insults, discrimination, and personal insults.

- Respect differences in technology, culture, and viewpoints.

## 2. Safety & Transparency
- Do not intentionally introduce malicious code, dangerous payloads, or destructive behavior.

- If you discover a vulnerability, report it via the security channel (Security Policy).

## 3. Technical Honesty
- Do not falsify test data, benchmarks, or simulation results.

- Do not hide errors or conceal technical risks.

## 4. Healthy cooperation
- Always try to resolve conflicts through discussion and technical analysis.
- Do not impose, do not use personal position to overwhelm others.

## 5. Do not politicize the project
AIC-Lab aims for sustainable AI research, beyond:
- political propaganda,
- corporate interests,
- social controversy.

Focus on science, technology and knowledge.

## 6. Commitment to the AIC philosophy
- Academic freedom
- Non-commercial
- Aiming for the long-term benefit of the community

---

Serious violations may lead to:
- warnings,
- limitation of contribution rights,
- or permanent bans depending on the case.

Let's build an open, humane and progressive environment together.
